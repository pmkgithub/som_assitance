som assistance is a sommelier and wine enthusiasts best friend.

Quickly and easily keep track of all your wine Tasting Events and Tasting Notes, all in one place.    With the powerful search functionality, you can find specific Tasting Notes in a matter of seconds.

Create Account:

Create an account here.

Once you have registered, only you will be able to create and search your personal wine Tasting Notes.

Create Tasting Notes:

Start by creating a Tasting Event.

Next, add Tasting Notes for each wine tasted at a Tasting Event.

Both Tasting Events and Tasting Notes may be edited at a later time.

Search Tasting Notes:

The true power of som assistance lies in its simple, yet powerful, search functionality.

Search by Primary Grape, Rating, and Price.

Search returns a list of Tasting Notes meeting your search criteria.
